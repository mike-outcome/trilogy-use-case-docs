Origin Flow Map

Where applicants moved from. The origin data is bimodal — roughly half local churn, half genuine in-migration across 24 states — so the view carries both scales at once.

Design rationale. A plain national choropleth collapses 47% of the population into a single dot over Memphis; a metro-only map discards the national story. The distance-band rail on the right is the primary encoding — the map is texture. Bands drive the filter; the map follows.
